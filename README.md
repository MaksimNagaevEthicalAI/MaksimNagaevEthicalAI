# Hi, I'm Maksim Nagaev 👋

Founder of **ALMA AI** — an independent research initiative exploring how artificial intelligence can become a transparent cognitive system for long-term psychological understanding.

Rather than building another conversational assistant, I am designing an evidence-based cognitive architecture that continuously constructs, evaluates, and refines psychological representations through structured reasoning.

---

# Current Research

ALMA investigates how AI can move beyond response generation toward genuine cognitive understanding.

Current research focuses on:

- Cognitive Architecture
- Evidence-Based Reasoning
- Knowledge Representation
- Psychological Modeling
- Cognitive State
- Reflection Systems
- Human Compatibility

---

# Philosophy

ALMA is built on a simple principle:

> Understanding should improve over time.

Instead of trying to predict the best response, ALMA continuously updates an evolving cognitive model using observations, patterns, hypotheses, evidence, confidence, uncertainty, memory, and reflection.

Every conversation becomes additional evidence rather than an isolated interaction.

# Current Development

The project is currently focused on research rather than implementation.

The primary objective is to establish a complete and internally consistent cognitive architecture before writing production code.

Current areas of development include:

- Designing cognitive components
- Defining cognitive objects
- Knowledge representation
- Evidence evaluation
- Probabilistic reasoning
- Confidence estimation
- Uncertainty management
- Memory architecture
- Reflection generation
- Prototype design

Implementation will begin only after the research architecture reaches sufficient maturity.

# Research Principles

Every design decision follows several fundamental principles:

- Evidence before conclusions
- Understand before advising
- Preserve uncertainty when evidence is insufficient
- Every hypothesis remains revisable
- Psychological understanding is never complete
- Transparency is more important than complexity
- Long-term understanding is more valuable than immediate answers

---

# Why ALMA?

Most conversational AI systems optimize for generating better responses.

ALMA explores a different question:

> Can artificial intelligence continuously improve its understanding of a person instead of simply improving its answers?

This question drives every architectural decision within the project.
